Eminem

Eminem, whose real name is Marshall Bruce Mathers III, is a multi-platinum selling rapper, songwriter, and record producer.
Often considered one of the greatest rappers of all time, he rose to mainstream popularity in the late 90s with his provocative lyrics and versatile flow.
His songs often touch on personal struggles, societal issues and controversies, while his alter-ego "Slim Shady" allows him to showcase a more outrageous and comedic side.
Eminem has won numerous awards and accolades for his music, including 15 Grammys and an Academy Award for Best Original Song.
